How do i change my password?

To change your password, go into My Account, select Change Password, fill in the form and your password will have changed.


How do i cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go into My Account, select Disable in the Auto Renewal section and the status of the auto Renewal should change to Off, once the Renewal date has been passed your subscription will end and you will revert back to a Lite Account.


How do i report a Product?

To report a product, go onto the Product Page of the product of concern, select the Report this Product Button, fill in the Report form and we shall recieve your report.


How can i request a product to Finicky?

To request a product to Finicky, let us know by going to the support section of the main menu, selecting contact us, fill in the form, Providing both the barcode if you have it and or the name of the product and pack size, e.g "Heinz Vegetable Soup 400G".
